Bio

Alicia Augello Cook Known as Alicia Keys, is an American song-writer. A classically trained pianist.
Keys begun composing songs by age of 12 and was signed at 15 years old by Columbia Records.
Keys has received numerous accolades in her career, including 15 competitive Grammy Awards and an award from the Songwriter Hall of Fame and National Music Publishers Association.
